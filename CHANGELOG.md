## Changelog for 1.0.0-beta.3

### **RRV Integration**
### **Mob Previews**
**Features & Improvements**
- Added full support for displaying "stacked" mobs in previews (like the Flare's stack of three Blazes).
- Mobs riding other mobs will now automatically scale to fit perfectly inside the preview box and rotate together smoothly as a single unit.
- Improved the visual alignment for stacked mobs that use custom player skins or skulls.

**Fixes**
- Fixed an issue where special horses (like the Headless Horseman's skeleton horse) would incorrectly show up as normal horses.
- Fixed riders not sitting correctly on their mounts within the preview window.
- Fixed a bug where custom skull items in recipe previews were rendering completely off-screen and appearing invisible.
