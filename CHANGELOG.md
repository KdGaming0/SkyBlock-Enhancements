## Changelog for 1.0.0-beta.4

### **RRV Integration**
**Added**
- Added Reforges to RRV! You can now view all Blacksmith Reforges and Reforge Stones in the recipe viewer.

### **Tooltip Enhancements**
**Added**
- Full Stack Price Modifier: Hold the bound key (default: Left Shift) to multiply displayed tooltip prices by the item's maximum stack size.
- Current Amount Price Modifier: Hold the bound key (default: Left Ctrl) to multiply displayed tooltip prices by the current stack amount being hovered over.
- Editable Keybindings: Both modifier keys are fully rebindable in the standard Minecraft Controls menu under the Skyblock Enhancements category.

**Fixes**
- Added Farming Tool type. Missing enhancements are now correctly shown on farming tools.
- Fixed a crash when RRV is not installed and Tooltip price data is on.
- Price data in tooltips is now on by default.

### **Other Enhancements**
- Optimized Item Glowing and Hide Item frames features.
