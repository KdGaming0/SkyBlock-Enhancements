## Changelog for 1.1.3

### **Skyblock Enhancements**
**New Features**
- Added slot locking. Hover any inventory slot and press **L** (changeable in Controls) to lock or unlock it. Items in a locked slot can't be moved, swapped out, or dropped — including with the drop key — so you won't lose them by accident. Locked slots show a small padlock, and your locks are saved separately for each of your Skyblock profiles and follow you correctly when you switch profiles.
- Added a setting to save your mouse cursor position between inventory screens. When enabled, reopening an inventory quickly will place the cursor back where it was, with a configurable time tolerance and an optional whitelist/blacklist for specific menu titles.
