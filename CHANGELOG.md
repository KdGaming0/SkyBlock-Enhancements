## Changelog for 1.1.9

### **Rarity Drop Guard**
**New**
- Added **Protect Rare Items From Dropping**. When turned on, items at or above a rarity you choose can't be dropped by accident — this covers the drop key and dragging an item out of an inventory. It's off by default; the default protected rarity is **Rare**.
- If you really do want to drop a protected item, keep **Allow Triple-Press To Drop** on (the default) and just press drop three times quickly on the same item. Turn that setting off to make rare items impossible to drop.
- Protection against dropping an item outside your inventory (by clicking or dragging it out) can be turned off on its own with **Also Protect Dragging Items Out**, if you only want the drop key protected.

### **Mining (Ping Offset)**
**Fixed**
- Fixed the mining overlay disappearing when you had the **Mining Speed Boost** pickaxe ability selected.
