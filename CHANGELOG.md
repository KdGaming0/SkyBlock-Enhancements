## Changelog for 1.1.8

### **Slot Locking & Binding**
**New**
- Locked items can now be dropped with the drop key, controlled by a new **Drop Locked Items With Q** setting. By default, this only works while you're in a dungeon, so you can still throw items to activate ability — but they stay protected everywhere else. Set it to **Always** to drop locked items anywhere, or **Never** to keep them fully locked.
